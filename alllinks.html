Children of Blood and Bone

Tomi Adeyemi

Free



About this book

Zelie, a young spirited adult, forced to bury her heritage, in order to survive in the cruel world forged by the lies of the king and his family. Ignorance befits evil. Zelie Adebola remembers when the soil of Orisha hummed with magic. Burners ignited flames, Tiders commanded waves and Zelie's Reaper mother summoned forth souls.

But everything changed the night magic disappeared, the night Zelie lost her mother.

Now Zelie has one chnace to bring maji back and strike against the monarchy. With the help of a rogue princess, and her older brother, Zelie must outwit and outrun the crown prince, who is hell-bent on validating his honor by eradicating magic for good.

Danger lurks in Orisha, yet the greatest danger may be Zelie as she struggles to control her powers-and her growing feelings for a certain enemy
